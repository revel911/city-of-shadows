# Hub: Shockoe Bottom

**Dominant Circle:** Mortalis
**Last updated:** 2026-04-22

---

## Flavor

Richmond's oldest and most fractured district. The slave trade ran through here. So did money, contraband, and secrets that the city has been trying to bury and rediscover in alternating decades ever since. Gentrification gnaws at the western edge — bars, brunch spots, converted tobacco warehouses. The eastern edge still belongs to the old families, the church community, and the things that live beneath the streets.

The mundane world presses hardest here. Which is exactly why the supernatural runs deepest. The Overwrite is most active in the Bottom — buildings that weren't here last month, addresses that don't match the map, a street sign that reads a name no one recognizes but everyone somehow knows.

**Tone:** investigative, heavy, historically saturated. Horror that comes from the weight of what was done here, not jump-scares.

---

## The Overwrite Manifestation

**Overwrite Effect:** Buried history — earlier drafts of the city surfacing, addresses that don't match any map.

**Active Anomalies:**
- Buildings appear and disappear over the span of weeks
- Street signs occasionally read names no one recognizes but everyone somehow knows
- A fully intact 19th-century street runs beneath the current one — cobblestones, signage, sealed entrance
- The Old Market memorial reads different names depending on when you look
- Construction keeps finding things the city has no record of

## Hub Moves

### Come Out of the Woodwork
When you make contact with the Bottom's old community networks — churches, mutual aid orgs, corner regulars — to find someone or something, roll with Mortalis.
- **10+:** Someone knows. They'll tell you — but they want to know why you're asking. Answer honestly and they give you everything.
- **7-9:** Someone knows, but getting to them costs you time, a favor, or exposure.
- **Miss:** Word that you were asking gets back to someone who would rather you didn't.

### Read the Layers
When you slow down and study a location in Shockoe Bottom for signs of the Overwrite — contradictions, anachronisms, things that don't match the record — roll with Mind.
- **10+:** The MC reveals one concrete discrepancy and one implication of what it means for the city. Take +1 forward acting on it.
- **7-9:** The MC reveals the discrepancy. Its meaning is yours to interpret.
- **Miss:** You find something that finds you back. The MC makes a hard move.

### Weight of the Dead
When you invoke the history of this place to compel someone to honesty, action, or stillness, roll with Spirit.
- **10+:** It lands. They are moved — act, speak, or stand down as you intend.
- **7-9:** It lands but costs you something: mark corruption, owe them a Debt, or leave yourself exposed.
- **Miss:** They are not moved. You are. The MC decides what stirs in you.

## Active Factions (Hub-Specific)

### The Revisionists
Mortalis faction operating informally through the city planning office and local historical societies. They know about the Overwrite. Their position: it must be managed, not stopped. They are quietly cataloguing discrepancies rather than correcting them. No one has asked whether management means containment or collusion.

- **Purpose / agenda:** Document and suppress Overwrite evidence in the public record
- **Structure:** Loose; three core members, a dozen Aware-adjacent contacts
- **Mood:** Controlled anxiety
- **Size / Strength:** Small but embedded in city infrastructure
- **Recent moves:** Filed an easement that delays development at the Old Market Site by 18 months. No stated reason.

### The Morrow Network
Not a formal faction — more a family business with supernatural tentacles. Celestine Morrow is the visible anchor. Declan is the bagman. They know where the Bottom's bodies are buried because their grandfather buried some of them. Neutral between Night and Mortalis because they have been doing business with both for three generations.

- **Purpose / agenda:** Survive and profit by remaining useful to all sides
- **Structure:** Family, extended by obligation
- **Mood:** Pragmatic
- **Size / Strength:** Small but deeply connected
- **Recent moves:** Declan recently led a supernatural entity into proximity with Nadia Vasek, resulting in a Scar. He owes Chris Caustes a Debt.
