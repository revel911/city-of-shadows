## MOVES

### Hunter Moves

**Prepared for Anything**
You have a well-stocked armory, full of modern and ancient weapons. Take another two custom weapons and select one additional add-on for every custom weapon you own.

**Watch Them Closely**
When you closely observe a supernatural person or creature, roll with Blood. On a hit, learn their nature and at least one unusual weakness or flaw; take +1 ongoing when you take advantage of these vulnerabilities. On a 10+, ask a follow-up question, answered honestly. On a miss, someone uses your diverted attention against you.

### Slasher / Avenger Moves

**Stand Firm**
When you Keep Your Cool or Let It Out while directly pursuing your mission, take +1 forward — unless your personal weakness is exploited.

**Unshakeable**
When you kill an enemy and make an example of the slain enemy, nearby enemies become frightened of you.

### Night Stalker Creed

**Banes**
When you go on a hunt, roll with Mind. On a 10+: hold 2. On 7-9: hold 1. On a miss: -1 ongoing for the hunt due to low morale. Spend hold 1-for-1 to:
- Arm yourself with a weapon that strikes at a specific Vampire or Werewolf weakness (+1 harm against that type).
- Gain +1 on defensive actions for the hunt.
- Protect yourself from being fed upon (name the form).
- Coat a weapon with Wolfsbane — force a transformed Werewolf back to human form.

## AVENGER UNDERTAKING

**Type:** Avenger

**Traits:**
- Your drive to kill is your defining purpose. Once you identify a target, they dominate your focus.
- Once per session, when you make progress toward eliminating a marked target — mark XP.
- You are the stuff of whispered legend. Every feared figure has a weakness.
- When you reveal your personal weakness to an enemy (not an ally) — mark XP.

**Personal Weakness:** Impatience when others get in the way of the mission. He will move without waiting. That is when he makes mistakes.

**The Mission:**
Johan was ordered to eliminate a target who turned out to be innocent — a woman his father's Stockholm cell had extracted and relocated off the books decades earlier. The order came from inside his network. Someone senior with access to the family's old operational records. He is still inside the network, still functional, running a parallel operation underneath.
