# Jacob Boone
## The Veteran — Werewolf: The Forsaken (Cahalith / Storm Lords)
Oregon Hill, Richmond VA

---

### IDENTITY
Male. 6'2", 220lbs. Built like a man who has absorbed punishment professionally and kept coming forward. Hands that look like they've been broken and reset. Fast for his size — always has been. Face that's hard to read until it isn't. Demeanor: contained. The kind of quiet that comes from having learned when to talk and when not to.

Background: Grew up poor in Oregon Hill. Boxing was the way out that didn't quite take him out. Retired from the ring eighteen months ago — the circumstances are his business. Six months ago something found him near the river and the world cracked open. He knows what he is now. He's working on being okay with it.
