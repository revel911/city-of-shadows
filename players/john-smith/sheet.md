## MOVES

### Spectre Moves

- [x] **Manifestation Transformation** (Orpheus / Skinrider) — Replaces Manifest. Naturally incorporeal; immune to physical weapons, affected only by supernatural or blessed weapons. When manifesting, roll with Spirit. On 10+: any Shade Form. On 7-9: First or Second Form only. On a miss: First Form only. Spend 2 harm to upgrade Form by 1 level.

- [x] **Skinrider Shade** — Skinrider Manifestation Forms:
  - *First Form:* Can speak briefly through others and force quick, jerky motions.
  - *Second Form:* Materializes as a humanoid of silver thread and cobwebs.
  - *Third Form:* Fully human, with highly-visible veins and arteries, skin too thin.

- [x] **Won't Be Ignored** — When you get in someone's way, take a 10+ instead of rolling. If you mislead, distract, or trick someone with an obvious supernatural display, roll with Spirit instead of Mind.

- [x] **Wall? What Wall?** — You always have an opening to escape a situation. You can choose an additional option off the list to bring someone with you, even if they would normally be unable to escape. On a miss, you and whoever you brought end up in the dangerous space between the worlds of the living and the dead.
