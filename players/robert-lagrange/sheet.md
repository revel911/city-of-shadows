## IDENTITY

**Full name:** Robert Lagrange
**Pronouns:** he/him
**Look:** Caucasian, appears late 30s, black hair with two grey streaks at the temples. Dark eyes that don't reflect light quite right. Moves like a man who learned long ago that stillness is a weapon.
**Demeanor:** Measured. The kind of quiet that makes other people fill the silence with things they shouldn't say.

**Background:** French-Canadian ex-cop, embraced 1931. Worked both sides during Prohibition — played the law and the smugglers against each other as long as he could. Drew a line at killing. Has never fully decided whether the Embrace was punishment or gift.

## KEY NPCs

**Elias Voss** — sire, Lasombra, Camarilla. Runs a private arbitration practice in Richmond. Respected. Not liked. Embraced Robert in 1931 for reasons he has never fully explained. Always holds a Debt on Robert. *Currently: owes Robert 1 Debt (2019 Camarilla testimony).*

**Cecile Mar** — Toreador, Night Circle. Vietnamese-French, mid-forties in appearance. Runs an art restoration studio in The Fan. Has been in Richmond since the 70s. Patient. Waiting for the right moment to call in her Debt. *Robert owes Cecile 1 Debt.*

**Darian Cross** — nominally mortal, Night Circle fixer. Mid-thirties, Black, broad-shouldered, always dressed like he's about to testify. Brokers information between Kindred and mortal criminal infrastructure. Never surprised. Never explained. *Robert owes Cross 1 Debt.*

**Marie-Claire Lagrange (nee Thibaudeau)** — DECEASED. Wife. Schoolteacher. Knew what Robert was doing and loved him anyway. Died 1947, natural causes. Robert was at her bedside. Did not let her see his face clearly at the end.
