# Hunter — Hunter: The Vigil

**Base Playbook (natural pairing):** The Hunter
**Prerequisite Move:** (see extension induction rules below)
**Key Mechanics:** Creeds define hunting focus with moves; Organizations grant specialized equipment and abilities; both grant gang benefits; expanded Advancement Chart.

---

This document contains the Hunter: The Vigil add-on rules for the Hunter archetype associated with Urban Shadows. Contents include:
- New Advancement Chart increasing advancements of your character (Corruption advancements are unaltered)
- 4 Hunter Creeds to join, each with 2 Hunter Conspiracies

Note: Hunters created with the Urban Shadows Hunter archetype do not automatically start with a Creed. It is up to the group and MC to decide if you want to start within a Creed; if so, give up your beginning archetype Move at the start of the game.

## Creeds
(Choose one only)

Joining a Hunter Creed confers both your Creed's abilities and the group/gang benefit simultaneously.

### Night Stalkers

While wizards and Demons are a problem, you see the beings that feed and hunt in the Night as your mortal enemies.

Trait: Many Night Stalker groups follow a standard procedure: observe, assess, report, move in and neutralize or capture. Task Force VALKYRIE tends to neutralize targets, while Cheiron prefers to harvest samples for medical research from either a live specimen or a corpse. Once per session, when you call in the cavalry to help with your actions, mark Experience. Even so, if plans go awry, it's your responsibility if you fail to follow orders — after all, this is your job, not your hobby. Once per session, if you stand firm on a plan even though it may put you in great danger, mark Experience.

[MOVE] Banes
You have studied the legends and modern data of the things you fight. When you go on a hunt, roll with Mind. On a 10+, hold 2. On a 7-9, hold 1. On a miss, take -1 ongoing for the hunt due to low morale. Spend hold 1-for-1 to:
- Choose a weapon that strikes at the Vampire or Werewolf's weakness. Name what it is (with MC approval) and increase the damage rating of that weapon by 1 harm toward either Vampires or Werewolves (not both). Multiple hold may be spent for additional weapons.
- Carry a talisman meant to ward away evil. For the duration of the hunt, gain +1 forward to Keep Your Cool or Turn to Violence when acting defensively.
- Carry an item that protects you from being fed upon in one form (Blood, emotions, or flesh) by a Vampire. Name what it is; for the duration of the hunt, you are immune to the Vampire's Eternal Hunger if it matches your protection.
- Coat your weapon (or ammunition) with Wolfsbane. Name that weapon; for the duration of the hunt, when you attack a Werewolf that has Transformed, they are forced back into human form.

[MOVE] Veteran Safe House
(Requires Safe House.)
You have a location that monsters don't know about. When you take this move, name it and choose 1:

- Torture Suite: You have a room stocked for extracting information from enemies. When you use this room, deal 1 harm on your victim and roll with Blood. On a hit, ask 1 question from the list below. On a 7-9, in addition to the question, mark corruption. You may mark additional corruption (up to two) to deal an extra 1 harm per corruption marked and ask an additional question each.
  - Tell me 1 weakness of your closest ally.
  - Tell me what you know of my organization.
  - What are your powers?
  - Who, besides me, could be considered your worst enemy?
  - Are you part of a group?
  - Where can I find any allies of yours?
  - Where can I find your most prized possession?
  - What is your darkest secret?
  After the first question has been answered, a PC may attempt to flee after any subsequent injury by escaping. Players who refuse to answer questions can take a Debt on you. Note: moves that allow lying when answering questions also apply here. If the injury inflicted is enough to kill the victim, they die and can no longer be questioned.

- Secret: You've taken steps to conceal your safe house — whether it's a trap door into a hidden basement or a camouflaged underground compound. This includes soundproofing. Only the most thorough searches will find it (MC's call).

- Medical Outpost: You have enough medical supplies stocked that healing is much easier. When you spend time in your safe house dressing wounds and injuries, you heal 1 extra harm per day on yourself and any individuals you shelter.

If you have Status 2+ in the Night Circle: when you study a supernatural enemy before engaging them, roll with Mind. On a 10+, you determine a specific weakness that can be exploited immediately. On a 7-9, you gain a general idea of a vulnerability but require further preparation. If you are inside your Safe House, this ability automatically succeeds without a roll.

## Night Stalker Organizations
(Choose one)

### Cheiron Group

The Cheiron Group is a conspiracy of hunters that operates as a company dedicated to capturing and experimenting on supernatural monsters to benefit their own technology and investments. To the public, Cheiron is merely another multinational business offering affordable medical supplies and pharmaceuticals. Inside the company, it is a different story. Cheiron's purpose is to gather and study as many supernatural creatures as possible — often with the same cold, uncaring manner they use when absorbing a smaller company into their conglomerate.

[MOVE] Regenerative Node
You have a piece of scar tissue from a regenerating being implanted somewhere on your body, with a shunt inserted into it. When pressure is applied, healing enzymes are secreted into your body, increasing your own healing speed. Once per session, you can Let It Out to add "rapidly heal 2 harm" to your list of options. On a miss, your injuries worsen and you cannot heal naturally, even while resting.

[MOVE] The Spinner's Fingers
You have spinnerets and silk glands taken from spider-like monsters implanted in your fingers, allowing you to create nearly invisible, steel-strength strands of spider silk. You can produce enough silk to entirely wrap a human-sized object, after which you must eat a large protein-rich meal to produce more. This silk is sticky to all beings except yourself and other spider-like creatures.

[MOVE] Plasmic Caul
Some Spectres form symbiotic bonds with humans — and when they are destroyed, they leave behind physical ectoplasmic residue. When you consume this residue and take in the emotional power of the Spectre, roll with Spirit. On a 10+, choose 3 emotions. On a 7-9, choose 1 emotion.
- Lust: Whatever the hunter is feeling at that moment, everyone nearby (close area) shares the exact same emotion.
- Anger: The hunter is protected from all damage of a single element type (fire, electricity, cold, etc.) for the remainder of the scene. Choose the element when this is selected.
- Shame: A zone of silence surrounds the hunter.
- Shock: The hunter emits a static discharge. Any electronic objects close to their face cease to work for the day.
- Sorrow: The hunter can hold their breath for 1 full scene.
- Fear: A sharp, cold, inconsistent wind whips around the hunter, carrying debris and knocking down enemies.

[MOVE] Evil Eye
When a Vampire is killed, a quick-witted hunter can extract its eye and take a piece of the undead's soul with it. The eye can then be implanted into a Cheiron hunter. When the operation is complete, the hunter is blind in the replaced eye (the MC may make a hard move on all perception-based rolls that don't come up a 10+). The eye also appears milky and bloated, giving the hunter -1 ongoing to Persuade, Mislead, or Figure Someone Out when the eye is visible. In return, the hunter can activate the eye once each nightfall and choose one of the following:

- Horrid Reality: The hunter projects traumatic hallucinations directly into a victim's mind. Roll with Heart. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
  - They end up in another dangerous yet very different situation.
  - They suffer harm on the way out.
  - They leave something important behind.
  - You learn how they break under pressure; take a Debt against them.

- Mesmerize: Using your voice to subtly influence a subject, you verbally implant a hypnotic suggestion in their subconscious. Roll with Mind. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 1. Spend 1 hold to make the target:
  - Speak a few words of your choice.
  - Give you something they hold.
  - Make a concerted attack on a target of your choice.
  - Truthfully answer one simple question.
  If you run out of hold, the power ends. If the target takes damage, you lose 1 hold. While the power is ongoing, to take any other action you must Keep Your Cool.
  If you have Status 3+ in the Mortalis Circle: you may spend 1 Debt owed to you to implant a single lasting suggestion that lingers beyond the initial interaction, causing the subject to act upon it later when a relevant trigger occurs.

- Dread Gaze: This power focuses the terror Vampires can inflict to insanely terrifying levels. Roll with Heart. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 1. Spend hold 1-for-1 to:
  - Inflict 1-harm (ap).
  - They take -1 right now.
  - You take +1 forward to persuade an NPC or Turn to Violence.
  If they fulfill your command, that counts for all remaining hold. On a miss, you inflict 1-harm (ap) on your subject to no benefit.

- Entrancement: When you have time and solitude with someone, they become fixated upon you. Roll with Heart. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 2. They spend your hold 1-for-1 by:
  - Giving you something you want.
  - Acting as your eyes and ears.
  - Fighting to protect you.
  - Doing something you tell them to.
  For NPCs, while you have hold over them they cannot act against you. For PCs, at any time you may spend your hold 1-for-1:
  - They Distract themselves with the thought of you (they must Keep Their Cool).
  - They inspire themselves with the thought of you (they take +1 forward right now).
  On a miss, they hold 2 over you on the exact same terms.
  When a new night falls and you choose a different power, you lose the previous one.
  If you have Status 2+ in the Mortalis Circle: when you entrance a mortal, they do not realize they are under your influence, even if an outside force tries to snap them out of it.

[MOVE] Berserker Splice
Research has shown that all Werewolves have a larger-than-normal medulla oblongata — the source of their natural rage. The Cheiron Group has experimented with transplanting that portion of the Werewolf brain into hunters. When you are threatened by another being with violence, roll with Blood. On a 10+, choose 3 positive effects and 2 negative effects. On a 7-9, choose 2 positive effects and 2 negative effects. On a miss, choose 1 positive effect and 2 negative effects, but you lose the ability to tell friend from foe and will attack either.

Positive Effects:
- Your melee attacks during the fight deal +1 harm.
- Your rage frightens everyone who sees it. Weak-willed characters (MC's call) run from you at first opportunity.
- Objects in your way are no obstacle. All of your actions for the fight gain the +Messy quality.
- Name a character on the scene but outside your reach. You cross the distance before they can adjust or react. (This option must be chosen when the move is rolled but can be held until the rage ends.)
- Name a character within your reach. While you keep fighting, you can intercept any attack directed at them and they suffer no damage.
- You are treated as having 1-armor (natural) for the duration of the rage.

Negative Effects:
- After the fighting has ceased, you collapse from exhaustion and wake at a time of the MC's choice.
- Take -1 ongoing to all non-Blood rolls for the remainder of the fight as the rage takes hold.
- The implant has altered your soul; mark corruption.
- The rage has consumed your body's store of calories. After the battle, take -1 Blood until you eat a large meal.

Advanced: On a 12+, you can take either 2 positive effects and no negative effects, or take all 3 positive effects for 1 negative.

If you have Status 3+ in the Night Circle: once per session when activating a Cheiron augmentation, choose one additional effect:
- Your Plasmic Caul emotions linger for an extra scene.
- Your Berserker Splice gains one extra positive effect.
When you use both augmentations in the same session, roll with Spirit. On a 10+, you control the surge of power completely. On a 7-9, your modifications briefly destabilize, causing mild side effects.

### Task Force Valkyrie

Task Force VALKYRIE is a top-secret United States government agency dedicated to hunting down and removing all traces of the unexplainable. While there are proper procedures, the best VALKYRIE agents have learned that survival sometimes requires cutting through the red tape. New agents are implanted with a special RFID chip that allows them to operate the Task Force's specialized arsenal — and also lets the organization track their members' locations at all times.

[MOVE] The Bleeder
This rifle-like weapon fires a focused micro-burst of energy that damages only Vampires, with the potential to eliminate some of their power. The effect on non-Vampires is nothing, but against a Vampire this weapon is a heavy hitter, causing the Blood in the area hit to burst from the body (2-harm, close/far). When you Turn to Violence with this weapon and roll a 12+, you also inflict a cumulative -1 ongoing until the target feeds.

[MOVE] Huginn Visor
You have a pair of specially treated black sunglasses. Any creature using supernatural means to persuade you finds the attempt immediately countered at a penalty of -3. NPC attempts (with the exception of especially powerful entities) are ignored rather than resisted. The drawback is that the wearer is prone to missing minor details in the surrounding area while these are worn.

[MOVE] Gleipnir Restraints
Resembling a thin strip of black plastic with a fingerprint panel on each end coded to a specific agent, these restraints use a kinetic feedback loop to turn the prisoner's own strength against them, restricting movement the more they struggle. Usually issued in sets of ten, one band can be used to tie a target's wrists or ankles. The challenge is subduing the target long enough to apply them. Once on, the bands will not snap through brute force — they must be cut by someone other than the captive.

[MOVE] Odin Reticle
(Requires Huginn Visor.)
Your treated sunglasses are now wired with a micro computer that helps you spot things trying to stay hidden. When you study a place of power, take +1 and ask one of the following questions in addition to any other questions granted by your roll:
- Is there anything actively hiding around me?
- Are there any incorporeal Spectres or beings in the area?
- What type of beings are within my vision? (MC should answer only beings the character would recognize, and note that only Werewolves, Vampires, and embodied Spectres register — all other archetypes appear as humans.)

[MOVE] Specialized Equipment
(Choose one.)

- Hod Rounds: Highly compressed wooden bullets with a mistletoe core. When fired, the bullet splinters, dealing -1 harm (minimum 1 harm). If the target is a Vampire, deal +1 harm instead. When you Turn to Violence on a 12+, your bullet strikes the Vampire's Heart, sending them into a death-like sleep called Torpor until the splinters are removed. (A PC can avoid this by successfully Escaping, though not necessarily from the entire situation.)

- Etheric Rounds: Bullets designed to hit Spectres, ghosts, Spirits, and anything else that normally cannot be hit. Against normal fleshy beings, these bullets deal -1 harm and inflict -1 ongoing for the scene. When used on a possessed person or on a Spectre, ghost, Spirit, or incorporeal being, they gain +1 harm and inflict -2 ongoing for the scene. The hunter must be able to see the being to shoot it.

- Frequency Pulse Emitter: A modified flashbang created to counter Werewolves. Activate and throw. When detonated, the grenade emits a high frequency inaudible to humans but devastating to Werewolves (and some Vampires with Auspex, MC's choice). Any Werewolf within close range is stunned and must revert to human form to preserve their hearing. Those in urhan (wolf) or urshul (near-wolf) form take -2 ongoing for the scene. Stunned characters cannot act physically for a short time; if they attempt to, the MC may also make a hard move.

## Spirit Slayers

There are others fighting the things in this world, letting you focus on the hunt for the truly unnatural: Demons and their spawn, the unknowable Fae.

Trait: Spirit Slayers have a much more liberal approach to tracking down monsters than other organizations — after all, their targets are not of the mortal realm. The Spirit Slayers have fought their share of battles against the Wild, but unlike many other hunter groups, they don't automatically assume that an individual is irredeemably evil. Once per session, if you contact a new group of non-mortals with the desire of expanding your contact list, mark Experience. Though these new contacts want to help, they bring baggage of their own and your relationship with them is not understood by your peers. When you are judged badly for your unusual contacts, mark Experience.

[MOVE] Blessed Item
You have an item blessed by a priest or holy man. When you go on a hunt, roll with Mind. On a 10+, whoever blessed this item is in favor and their god favors you as well; take +1 forward and choose 1. On a 7-9, you can tell your item is losing its effectiveness and should be returned to the priest soon; choose 1.
- All those against you will likely flee in fear. Any enemy of this item's god who is touched will be cast into the Spirit realm for one session.
- The item is a weapon. When you inflict an injury on a member of Wild, increase the damage by +1 harm.
- The item is a talisman or armor. You get +1 armor against members of Wild.

[MOVE] Holy Ground
You know a place that Demons and Spirits refuse to enter — usually a church. Name what and where it is. When you go there, monsters may not follow. Note that this place is likely not stocked for a prolonged visit.

## Spirit Slayer Organizations
(Choose one)

### Ascending Ones

The Ascending Ones are a conspiracy of hunters that originally formed in Egypt to fight against the monsters of darkness, continuing their efforts to this day while keeping their activities — and their prey — from reaching ordinary people. Members not only keep creatures of darkness from attacking the innocent, but ensure the innocent stay unaware of the dark powers lurking around them. The Ascending Ones are as likely to talk a creature out of its actions as to attack it outright, particularly if that creature is known to be non-threatening to humans. They will even perform acts of diplomacy between rival monster factions, but stand ready to take action against both parties should peaceful means fail. The organization retains traces of its origins as platoons of holy soldiers in Africa and the Middle East — a heritage tempered by ties to organized crime and the drug trade, which they continue to use as a means of gaining resources and contacts.

[MOVE] Eye of Ra
Eye of Ra is sandalwood oil infused with sacred herbs and plants that sharpen the senses. It also contains kohl, the cosmetic traditionally used by Egyptians to darken their eyelids, which contains several toxic compounds. The oil is applied by dabbing it around the eye in the shape of the sacred symbol of the Eye of Ra. When you do, roll with Spirit. On a hit, hold 1. You can spend this hold to ask the MC any one question about your surroundings. On a 10+, you also pierce the veil and can perceive any ghost, spirit, or other incorporeal being. On a miss, the chemicals seep into your blood, causing an adverse effect determined by the MC.

[MOVE] Breath of Ma'at
Hunters must undertake actions that righteous people would consider sinful. These acts weigh heavily upon the soul — but Ma'at, the goddess of truth and divine justice, knows that the hunter's cause is virtuous and that all may be forgiven. Light the incense, meditate, and roll with Spirit. On a 10+, you may remove all marked corruption accumulated for your current advancement. On a 7-9, erase 1 corruption but take -1 ongoing for the scene as if drugged. On a miss, the incense smoke backfires, causing an adverse effect determined by the MC.

[MOVE] Bennu-Bird Feather
Supposedly plucked from the tail of the Bennu-bird — the Egyptian phoenix associated with Ra — this feather is ground up with medicinal herbs and potent opiates into a thick paste that accelerates healing tremendously. It must be smeared over a wound to have any effect. The unguent smells strongly of cardamom and willowbark with an acrid, medicinal tang. When used, roll with Spirit. On a 10+, the target heals 3 harm. On a 7-9, the target heals 1 harm. On a miss, the patient is poisoned, taking 2 harm (ap) and -2 ongoing for the scene. Note: this move cannot be used in the middle of most scenes unless time is taken to properly apply the salve.

[MOVE] Blood of the Cobra
An exotic compound of cobra venom, hashish, and various alchemical reagents brewed into a devastatingly toxic elixir. It must be injected directly into the vein; most hunters use their inner arm for quick access during battle. When you inject the elixir, roll with Blood. On a 10+, you gain +1 Blood that ignores the human limit for a scene, and any being that drinks your blood takes 1 harm (ap). On a 7-9, choose either Turn to Violence or Escape; you gain +1 ongoing when you use that move for the scene, and any being that drinks your blood takes 1 harm (ap). On a miss, the chemicals backfire, causing an adverse effect; mark a Scar or take -1 ongoing.

[MOVE] Mesmeric Vapors
In their non-smoked form, Mesmeric Vapors resemble loose tobacco with a subtly sweeter scent. They are smoked like a pipe or cigarette. The smoke given off is a pale golden color and hangs with an unnatural heaviness in the air, remaining still even in a moderate breeze. After you've smoked the Vapors, you can breathe them out to plant a command inside a target's head. Roll with Mind. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 1. Spend hold 1-for-1 to:
- They give you something you want.
- They act as your eyes and ears.
- Inflict 1-harm (ap).
- They take -1 ongoing until they leave the vapors.
If they fulfill your command, that counts for all remaining hold. On a miss, you inflict 1-harm (ap) on both your subject and yourself to no benefit. This injury cannot be healed until the person has left the vapors.

### Lucifuge

The Lucifuge is a conspiracy of hunters composed of humans descended from Demons who have vowed to use their demonic powers to fight the monsters in the dark. These creatures are carefully tracked and studied before the Seventh Generation reveals themselves to destroy them. They are particularly effective at tracking down Demons and demon-born, and possess a surprising amount of information on angels. Members can be born into the Lucifuge as children of demon-blood; if their blood is known but their families do not follow the ways of the Lucifuge, potentials will be approached by members when their powers manifest. At any point the Lucifuge and the Seventh Generation have exactly 666 members — if one dies, another always seems ready to take their place.

Note: Unlike most other Hunter Organizations that can only originate from the Hunter archetype, the Lucifuge may also originate from the Tainted archetype. During advancement, the only difference is that instead of taking "Hunter moves," you will be taking "Tainted Moves."

[MOVE] Infernal Senses
Your demonic senses are well developed, allowing you to use visions or intuition to gain insight into situations and people. By using your senses to lay a lasting mark on a person — just as God did to Cain — roll with Spirit. On a 10+, hold 2. On a 7-9, hold 1 and mark corruption. On a miss, you are visited by searing visions of hell's torment. Spend hold to:
- Spend 1 hold: If you Let It Out, treat a 7-9 as a 10+ and a miss as a 7-9.
- Spend 1 hold: You may add these questions to the list when you Figure Someone Out.
  - Has this person committed any serious sins recently?
  - Is this person a member of Wild?
- Spend 1 hold and take 1 harm (ap) to place a visible mark on someone. The mark cannot be removed by normal means (MC's call as to what could remove it).

[MOVE] My Father's Wrath
Even though you turned your back on your heritage as a child of Lucifer, you can still call upon their power and strength. Roll with Blood. On a 10+, hold 2. On a 7-9, hold 1 and mark corruption. On a miss, the flames of your father's torment affect you as well. Spend hold to:
- Spend 1 hold: Summon hellfire and attack at close range (2-harm, close).
- Spend 1 hold: Increase the damage of one hand-range attack by +1 harm.
- Spend 1 hold: Take on an aspect of demonic form (wings, a tail, horns, etc.) for the scene. Once chosen, you cannot spend hold to add additional aspects.

[MOVE] Silver Tongue
Just as your father persuaded Eve to bite the apple, you are more persuasive — and more. You can understand and speak any language for a short time, and can curse others. When you use this on others, roll with Heart. On a 10+, hold 2. On a 7-9, hold 1 and mark corruption. On a miss, your tongue is confused and you can only speak gibberish for a time determined by the MC, never longer than a day. Spend hold to:
- Spend 1 hold: Gain +1 forward when you try to Persuade or Mislead someone. Multiple hold may be spent for greater effect.
- Spend 1 hold: For the scene, you can speak and understand any language.
- Spend 1 hold: Double the get-in-the-way penalty or counter one move that does not require preparation. The target must be able to hear your curse.

## Witch Finders

Absolute power corrupts absolutely. Those from the Power Circle cannot be trusted because they are easily corrupted by the powers they draw from outside sources.

Trait: Beings of magic and fear exist because they are the iniquitous servitors of evil. Whenever you take harm when smiting Power in God's name, mark Experience. Witch Finders can certainly show mercy — the whole Creed comes, after all, from the love of a merciful God. If you take an aggressive action against a repentant witch or other member of Power — even if the repentance is feigned — or without giving the enemy a chance to repent, mark Experience.

[MOVE] Disbelief
It's all illusions and parlor tricks. You hold power in the fact that you simply don't believe in things like magic — which is ironic, given that you fight it. Roll with Spirit. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 1. On a miss, you make yourself more vulnerable to the spells and powers of the Power Circle. Spend hold to counter and/or negate 1 spell or move used by a member of the Power Circle.

[MOVE] Inquisition
Sometimes the old ways are just more effective. Rather than persuading someone through kind words, torturing helps extract information more quickly and more completely. When you Figure Someone Out using torturing techniques from either improvised or specialized tools, you gain +1 additional hold and are allowed to ask questions not on the list. You may also Persuade or Mislead at +2 Forward. You accept that the price of information is worth the cost of your soul; mark corruption.

## Witch Finder Organizations
(Choose one)

### Knights of Saint George

The Knights of Saint George is a conspiracy of hunters particularly dedicated to eradicating witches, thanks to the influence of their founder. Although they are tied to the Church of England, their beliefs come from a much earlier time, linking them to far more primitive and powerful gods. Any member must be inducted. Base members are Squires, who run the day-to-day operations and fronts for the conspiracy. To become a Knight, the potential must undergo the first of the Three Revelations — a process that may be undertaken at any point in their life. The Three Revelations are: that wizards, witches, and creatures of darkness exist; that the Knights do not follow Christian beliefs but instead work to appease faceless creatures so the world will not end; and that there are powerful secrets the Knights know, but learning them brings the attention of the darkness.

[MOVE] The Gospel of Agares
The Gospel of Agares deals with confronting sorcery directly — nullifying magic and retaliating against those who wield it. When you invoke the Gospel of Agares, roll with Spirit. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 2 and mark corruption. On a miss, a powerful entity notices your interference; the MC makes a hard move on their behalf and they take a Debt against you. Spend hold 1-for-1 to:
- Nullify: Ignore one supernatural ability or Power Circle move directed at you or your allies. This does not negate the effect entirely — it simply does not affect you. (e.g., a fireball still burns the area but leaves you untouched.)
- Aegis: Gain +1 armor against supernatural attacks for the scene.
- Retribution: When you nullify a supernatural attack, inflict 2-harm (ap) on the source of the ability (if applicable).
- Hunter's Flame: Mark a target with an ethereal flame visible only to you, allowing you to track them unerringly until the scene ends.

[MOVE] The Gospel of Amon
The Gospel of Amon exploits the corruption and weaknesses of the magical and supernatural, turning their power against them. When you invoke the Gospel of Amon, roll with Spirit. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 2 and mark corruption. On a miss, a powerful entity notices your interference; the MC makes a hard move on their behalf and they take a Debt against you. Spend hold 1-for-1 to:
- Corrupting Strike: When a member of the Power Circle uses a supernatural ability within your presence, inflict 1-harm (ap) on them.
- Thief of Power: Steal a spell or supernatural move from a member of the Power Circle for one scene. They cannot use it while you retain it.
- Witchbane: Force a member of the Power Circle to mark corruption when they use a supernatural ability in your presence.
- Proclaimed Weakness: Declare a specific weakness (e.g., cold iron, sunlight) for a supernatural creature. For the remainder of the scene, that weakness applies to them.

[MOVE] The Gospel of Beleth
The Gospel of Beleth disrupts the gathering and wielding of supernatural power and forces submission through fear. When you invoke the Gospel of Beleth, roll with Spirit. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 2 and mark corruption. On a miss, a powerful entity notices your interference; the MC makes a hard move on their behalf and they take a Debt against you. Spend hold 1-for-1 to:
- Power Dispel: Prevent a member of the Power Circle from gaining hold or charges from their supernatural moves.
- Spectral Awareness: Sense all members of the Power Circle within close range, even those who are invisible, incorporeal, or hiding through magical means.
- Exhortation: Compel a supernatural enemy to cease their aggression and stand down for one action or moment.
- Sin of Sloth: Curse a member of the Power Circle with lethargy, giving them -1 forward to their next roll.
- Poisoned Source: Whenever they use a supernatural ability in your presence, they suffer 1-harm (ap).

### Malleus Maleficarum

Malleus Maleficarum is a conspiracy of hunters that are a secret branch of the Catholic Church dedicated to using holy powers to eradicate creatures of darkness. Quite simply, the Malleus Maleficarum wants to stop supernatural creatures and the horrors they inflict on their flock. To that end, they call on the powers of God and the blessings of His saints. Any malevolent monster that threatens those in their care — and the Malleus will come running.

Note: Unlike most other Hunter Organizations that can only originate from the Hunter archetype, the Malleus Maleficarum may also originate from the Hallowed archetype. During advancement, the only difference is that instead of taking "Hunter moves," you will be taking "Hallowed Moves."

[MOVE] His Wrath
Your prayers call down blessings that aid you on the attack. Roll with Spirit. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 2 and mark corruption. Spend hold to:
- Spend 1 hold: Bless a weapon for the scene, giving it +1 harm. The damage type also gains +Holy. You can only bless each weapon once per scene, but may spend additional hold to bless extra weapons.
- Spend 1 hold: Cause every member of Power in the area to suffer 1-harm (close). Multiple hold may be spent to increase damage by +1 harm per extra hold.
- Spend 1 hold: Call a blessing to aid yourself or another in a time of need. You or another player may take +1 forward. Multiple hold may be spent to increase this effect.

On a miss, choose 2:
- Mark corruption.
- Take 2-harm (ap).
- Take a cumulative -1 ongoing until you spend a day in fasting and prayer.
- The MC can make a hard move.

[MOVE] His Mercy

### Aegis Kai Doru

Aegis Kai Doru is a conspiracy of hunters who are able to effectively use the treasures the Aegis holds in its power. Their one motivation: collect the powerful relics of ages past and keep them away from humans who align themselves with the arcane or the evil of the night. While Aegis members are dedicated to their cause, they do not go into missions blindly — realizing that sometimes keeping a foul target alive can be just as effective as striking them dead. Potential Guardians must undergo a trial called the First Initiation, where they are given hallucinogens and left alone at sunset in a vast labyrinth. To successfully complete the trial, they must find the exit before dawn. Upon doing so, they may take the Vow of the Sword and become a true member.

[RELIC] Ringsel
When a truly enlightened Buddhist master dies and the body is cremated, small pearl-like stones are sometimes found among the ashes. Tibetan Buddhism considers these Ringsel to be the physical embodiment of the master's wisdom, knowledge, and enlightenment. These stones have two handy uses. First, if you have a Ringsel on your person when you would mark corruption, you may instead take 2-harm (ap). Second, when you meditate over a Ringsel for a few hours, you may heal your wounds. [RELIC] Skeleton Key
