VAMPIRE: THE MASQUERADE -- ADD-ON RULES

INFORMATION
 
Here you will find the Vampire the Masquerade add-on rules and text for the Vamp archetype associated with Urban Shadows. You can expect to find a new Advancement Chart increasing advancements of your character (Corruption advancements are unaltered), 14 Clans from the 3 opposing Sects, and 17 Disciplines with a variety of powers each entails.
 
Note: Vampires created with the Urban Shadows Vamp archetype do not automatically start in a Clan or with any Clan Disciplines. It is up to the group and MC to decide if you want to start in a Clan; if so, you give up your beginning archetype Move to be inducted into a Clan and take a Discipline at the start of the game.
 
 
CLANS
(Only able to choose one)
 
Joining a Vampire Clan confers not only your Clan's Disciplines but also the group/gang benefit at the same time.
 
 
BANU HAQIM
 
The Banu Haqim are warrior-philosophers, balancing bloodshed with a strict code of justice. Their lineage traces back to Haqim, the first judge and assassin. Their loyalty lies in contracts and their word is their bond, but they are feared for their deadly efficiency. Shadows and blood are both their weapons and their sacrifice.
 
Trait: Law of Judgment -- Mark Experience when you kill a Night Circle member justifiably or protect a Mortalis Circle member. Their blades serve both vengeance and justice.
Sect: Independent or Camarilla, acting as enforcers, assassins, and arbitrators. They respect power but demand honor.
Disciplines: Blood Sorcery, Celerity, and Obfuscate.
 
 
BRUJAH
 
The Brujah are rebels, philosophers, and warriors who burn with passion and rage. Once great thinkers and visionaries, they now wage wars for freedom against oppressive structures. They are quick to inspire but quicker to fight, living for a cause that may never come.
 
Trait: Anarchist -- Once per session, if you choose corruption over peace, mark Experience. Their ideals are as fiery as their tempers.
Sect: Predominantly Anarch, though some remain in the Camarilla as dissidents. Their rebellion echoes through the centuries.
Disciplines: Celerity, Potence, and Presence.
 
 
GANGREL
 
Gangrel are primal survivors, deeply attuned to nature and the call of the wild. Their existence is raw and untamed, and they often prefer the company of beasts to that of other Kindred. They embrace their monstrous nature, wearing their feral scars proudly.
 
Trait: Primal Domination -- Gain experience when you dominate through force or reject technological aid. Their instincts are their guide and their shield.
Sect: Most join the Anarchs or wander as loners, rejecting the politics of civilization. They respect only those who respect the wild.
Disciplines: Animalism, Fortitude, and Protean.
 
 
HECATA
 
The Hecata are death's merchants -- necromancers who rule both the living and the dead. Their clan is built on blood pacts and betrayal, united by a shared mastery of Oblivion. To them, death is not an end but a tool and a currency.
 
Trait: Excruciating Kiss -- Gain experience when feeding to cause pain or using wealth to dominate. Death and power flow through their veins.
Sect: Independent, though they sell their services to Camarilla and Anarch alike. Their loyalty is to the dead and their family.
Disciplines: Auspex, Fortitude, and Oblivion.
 
 
LASOMBRA
 
Lasombra are shadows given form -- ruthless manipulators who command darkness itself. Their history is steeped in cruelty and conquest, and they scorn the weakness of humanity. With no reflection, they live knowing their every move echoes in darkness.
 
Trait: Vampiric Superiority -- Mark Experience when asserting dominance or using shadows to intimidate. Their presence bends light and souls.
Sect: Recently defected to the Camarilla, though many remain Sabbat. They command fear and rarely tolerate disobedience.
Disciplines: Dominate, Oblivion, and Potence.
 
 
MALKAVIAN
 
The Malkavians walk the edge of madness and prophecy, their fractured minds serving as both gift and curse. Their insight can be terrifyingly clear or disastrously destructive. They are cursed to see truths that others cannot.
 
Trait: Insane Insight -- Once per session, when you Figure Someone Out using madness, mark Experience. Their visions guide and haunt them.
Sect: Loyal to the Camarilla or running wild among the Anarchs. Their madness is their weapon and their prison.
Disciplines: Auspex, Dominate, and Obfuscate.
 
 
THE MINISTRY
 
The Ministry, once known as the Followers of Set, are a clan of deceivers, liberators, and tempters. They believe that truth is a prison, and only through breaking taboos and embracing the hidden darkness within can one achieve enlightenment. While many of their kind operate in religious or cult-like structures, others infiltrate mortal organizations and undermine them from within. The Ministry thrives on corruption -- both moral and physical -- twisting others to their cause with whispers of freedom and indulgence. In modern nights, they have shed their more overt religious trappings to blend into the shadows of mortal vices, operating through crime, politics, and underground movements. Their followers may not always recognize themselves as part of a cult, but once in the Ministry's web, there is no escape.
 
Trait: Spreading Corruption -- Gain experience when you convince another to act against their moral instincts.
Sect: The Ministry exists outside traditional sects, infiltrating both the Camarilla and the Anarchs as they see fit, though their chaotic nature makes them unwelcome in more rigid power structures.
Disciplines: Presence, Protean, and Obfuscate.
 
 
NOSFERATU
 
Monsters, spies, and the unseen eyes of the night, the Nosferatu are cursed with grotesque forms that hide their true power: knowledge. Living in sewers and forgotten places, they weave a web of secrets and shadows where whispers become weapons. Disfigured by their Embrace, they have long traded beauty for power, turning society's rejection into their greatest advantage. Their loyalty is rare, but their vengeance is cold and absolute.
 
Trait: Underworld Knowledge -- Gain experience when you exploit secrets.
Sect: Camarilla's hidden eyes or the Anarchs' shadowed informants.
Disciplines: Animalism, Obfuscate, and Potence.
 
 
RAVNOS
 
Tricksters, vagabonds, and illusionists, the Ravnos craft their existence from deceit and adventure. Rootless and elusive, they roam from city to city leaving chaos and confusion in their wake. Their lives are bound to stories -- half-truths, cons, and legend -- yet behind every falsehood lies a glimmer of insight. They are survivors, always one step ahead of the hunter and one lie from their undoing.
 
Trait: Compulsive Liar -- Gain experience when deception costs you trust.
Sect: Nomadic and Independent, driven by their own chaos.
Disciplines: Animalism, Obfuscate, and Presence.
 
 
SALUBRI
 
Healers, seers, and martyrs, the Salubri are Kindred of rare empathy, walking a path of sacrifice where others walk in shadow. Their third eye marks them as both blessed and cursed, for their kindness often brings persecution. Few survive long in the night, hunted by those who fear their power and purity. Yet they persist, offering solace to the suffering and standing as guardians against the darkness.
 
Trait: Martyr's Insight -- Gain experience when you heal at a cost.
Sect: Often hunted, they travel alone or find rare refuge with the Anarchs.
Disciplines: Auspex, Fortitude, and Blood Sorcery.
 
 
TOREADOR
 
The Toreador are patrons, poets, and hedonists who chase beauty with an obsessive hunger. They revel in the ecstasy of creation and destruction, crafting legacies in art, love, and ruin. Yet their passion is a curse -- what inspires them today may bore them tomorrow. Beneath their elegance is the agony of the eternal critic, desperate for meaning in an endless night.
 
Trait: Artistic Obsession -- Gain experience when beauty enthralls you.
Sect: Camarilla's charm or the Anarchs' hedonists.
Disciplines: Auspex, Celerity, and Presence.
 
 
TREMERE
 
Scholars of the arcane and masters of blood magic, the Tremere built their power through ritual, theft, and conquest. Once human sorcerers, they now shape the world with vitae, binding power with ancient oaths and sigils. Yet their loyalty is fractured and their ambition knows no bounds. Knowledge is their weapon and their curse, for every spell exacts a price.
 
Trait: Blood Sorcery -- Gain experience when your magic costs you dearly.
Sect: Camarilla's sorcerers, with splintered heretics abroad.
Disciplines: Auspex, Dominate, and Blood Sorcery.
 
 
TZIMISCE
 
Tyrants and fleshcrafters, the Tzimisce are creatures of both elegance and monstrosity. They shape bone, flesh, and will as easily as others shape clay. Their cruelty is matched only by their pride, for they believe in absolute mastery -- of themselves, of others, and of the land. Home is not where they dwell, but where they rule.
 
Trait: Horrific Visage -- Gain experience when your monstrous nature evokes fear.
Sect: Sabbat masters or solitary horrors.
Disciplines: Protean, Dominate, and Oblivion.
 
 
VENTRUE
 
The Ventrue are kings, conquerors, and CEOs -- leaders who bend the world to their will. Wealth, power, and tradition are their birthright, and they rule with an iron fist wrapped in velvet. Their blood demands only the finest, and their pride tolerates no insult. Failure is fleeting, but victory is eternal.
 
Trait: Blue Blood Authority -- Gain experience when you enforce power through debt.
Sect: Camarilla's sovereigns, ruling with silver and steel.
Disciplines: Dominate, Fortitude, and Presence.
 
 
DISCIPLINES
(Disciplines are associated with particular Clans)
 
 
ANIMALISM
 
The Discipline of Animalism allows the Vampire to develop a close, intense connection with their primordial nature. Animals grow distinctly agitated in the presence of a Vampire who lacks this Discipline.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Feral Whispers: The Vampire creates an empathic connection with beasts. When you rely on your animal instincts to make sense of a charged situation, roll with Blood. On a 10+, ask the MC three questions. If you act on one of the MC's answers, add 1 to your first roll. On a 7-9, ask one:
- Where is my best Escape route or way in?
- Which enemy is the most vulnerable to me?
- What is their secret weakness?
- What poses the biggest threat to me?
- Who is in control here?
 
[DISCIPLINE] Drawing Out the Beast: When you release your Beast, roll with Blood. On a 10+, transfer up to 2 Corruption to a target. On a 7-9, transfer 1 Corruption but the Beast lashes out unpredictably. On a miss, mark 1 Corruption and the MC makes a hard move. If the transfer is to a PC, they record the transferred Corruption and mark a Debt against you. If the transfer is to an NPC, the NPC takes 1 harm for 1 Corruption or 2 harm for 2 Corruption (ap close).
 
[DISCIPLINE] Call of the Wild: The Beast within you is not only a predator -- it is a herald of the Wild itself. When you call upon the chaotic essence of nature, roll with Spirit. On a 10+, the Wild answers; choose two:
- Summon a swarm or pack of creatures native to the area (wolves, crows, rats, insects, etc.) that follow a simple command.
- Invoke a supernatural territorial claim -- for the rest of the scene, all Beasts, supernatural predators, and Wild-touched creatures instinctively recognize you as an apex being.
- Tap into the Wild's untamed power -- for the rest of the scene, your voice and presence carry unnatural weight over animals and Wild creatures, allowing you to issue simple commands that they struggle to disobey.
On a 7-9, the Wild stirs but at a cost. Choose one, and the MC will introduce a complication (a Fae trickster notices you, a predator arrives instead of allies, or the Wild demands a price). On a miss, the Wild rejects your call -- or worse, something from the deep shadows answers instead. Mark 1 Corruption and the MC makes a hard move.
 
 
AUSPEX
 
This Discipline bestows uncanny sensory abilities upon the Vampire.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Heightened Senses: When you Figure Someone Out, always ask one extra question, even on a miss.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Aura Perception: You sense others' emotional states. When you do, roll with Mind. On a 10+, you perceive the intensity and dominant type (anger, sorrow, love, confusion, etc.) of others' emotions as clearly as you can see the color and details of their clothes. On a 7-9, you can perceive either intensity or type, but not both. On a miss, you can perceive either intensity or type, but others nearby also sense your own dominant emotion without consciously knowing why they are so certain about you. Aura sensing lasts a few minutes per effort.
If you have Status 2+ in the Night Circle, when you use Aura Perception, your connection lasts for the entire scene instead of a brief moment.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Telepathy: The Vampire projects a portion of their consciousness into a nearby mortal's Mind, creating a mental link through which they can communicate wordlessly or read the target's deepest thoughts. When you perform a deep scan to uncover thoughts or memories protected by the subconscious, roll with Mind. If physical contact can be made (through intimacy or by touching a helpless target), add +1 and receive one additional question. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 1. On a miss, the target gains 2 hold against you; if they are in your Circle, they can spend hold to trigger Debts or lower your Circle Status. Spend your hold to ask the player or MC questions, 1 for 1:
- What thoughts have consumed the majority of the character's time?
- What secret pains or regrets does the character harbor?
- What plan or plans is the character constructing or initiating in the next few days?
- For what does the character seek forgiveness?
- In what ways is the character mentally vulnerable?
- What is the character's current attitude toward me?
- Where has the character recently been that they wish to keep secret?
On a miss, they hold 2 over you on the exact same terms.
[ADVANCED] On a 12+, you can completely recall memories that the target has recently experienced in place of any questions.
If you have Status 3+ in the Night Circle, when you use Telepathy you may subtly imprint an emotion into the target's subconscious, influencing their actions without them realizing why.
 
 
BLOOD SORCERY
 
When you choose the Blood Sorcery Discipline, choose your primary path and take a power from that path. The Path of Blood is the most common thaumaturgical path. As a standard advance, you can take an additional path and gain a power from that path. In order to take a new path beyond your first, you must spend time with another Tremere who teaches you that knowledge; they take 1 Debt on you.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Taste for Blood: This power was developed as a means of testing a foe's might. Whenever you touch the blood of a subject, roll with Mind. On a hit, take +1 forward to your next interaction with the subject. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 1 and mark corruption. On a miss, your psychic read gets reversed and the MC gets 1 hold on the subject's behalf. Spend hold 1 for 1 to ask a question from the list below:
- What is the subject's attitude toward me?
- What is the subject's weakness?
- Who do they hold a Debt over?
- Who holds a Debt over them?
- What is their approximate location?
If you have Status 3+ in the Power Circle, you may choose one additional effect when spending hold:
- Briefly disrupt the subject's supernatural abilities for a scene.
- Reveal whether the subject has an active magical effect on them.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Theft of Vitae: (requires Taste for Blood) You can siphon the blood from a subject within a short distance. Roll with Blood. On a 10+, hold 2. On a 7-9, hold 1 and mark corruption. On a miss, the subject gains a Debt on you or triggers a Circle Turn event. Spend hold 1 for 1 to:
- Take +1 forward.
- Reduce 1 injury.
- Subject takes 1 harm.
- Weaken your subject into a submissive position.
You can instead use both hold to heal an ally 1 harm.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Lure of Flames: This path grants you the ability to conjure forth mystical flames. Roll with Spirit. On a hit, you inflict harm. On a 10+, hold 2. On a 7-9, hold 1 and mark corruption. On a miss, you lose control of the flames. Spend hold 1 for 1 to:
- Inflict 2 harm (+injury level).
- Summon a large fire (+area).
- Maintain control of the flames.
- Cause the flames to burn out after affecting your target.
If you have Status 2+ within the Power Circle, you may spend 1 additional hold to ignore supernatural fire resistance for a target.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Animate the Unmoving: Objects affected by this power move as the Vampire dictates. By concentrating, you can impart an animating force to another object within close range and treat it as an extension of yourself. When you animate an object, roll with Mind. On a 10+, you can control the object like a part of yourself and gain +1 ongoing on any action with it. On a 7-9, you can control the object like a part of yourself. On a miss, you suffer 1 harm as a bit of your life essence leaks out.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Verdant Haven: With access to a sufficient amount of plant matter, you can craft a magical shelter. Roll with Mind. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 2. On a miss, hold 1 but your structure is imperfect in some way the MC will describe. Spend hold 1 for 1:
- The structure is big enough for 3 others.
- You camouflage the structure.
- It has a healing aura; those who rest in it reduce an injury by 1 level.
- The occupants are protected from the elements.
- It is nearly impenetrable (+2 armor).
 
[DISCIPLINE] Prison of Water: You can command a sufficiently large quantity of water to animate and imprison a subject. Roll with Mind. On a 10+, hold 2. On a 7-9, hold 1 but it will only last a moment. On a miss, your water takes on a mind of its own. Spend hold 1 for 1:
- Hold a target in place, unable to move or physically attack.
- Block the target's line of sight.
- Drown them (+1 harm ap close).
 
[DISCIPLINE] Weather Control: (requires Prison of Water) You can control the weather. Roll with Mind. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 1. On a miss, the weather is uncontrolled and wild. Spend hold 1 for 1:
- Widespread -- you affect a large area (+far).
- Summon fog -- create a veil of mist that allows you to move about unseen and unheard as long as you give yourself proper distance from those looking for you. When a PC attempts to Figure Someone Out or Study a Place of Power near your sanctum, they take -1.
- High winds -- everyone in the area takes +1 armor versus all ranged attacks.
- Thunder and lightning -- deal 2 harm ap loud to a single target.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Poison the Well of Life: (only for Clan Banu Haqim) The master of this Quietus power can taint blood, converting it into a deadly poison against other Vampires who drink from the mortal whose blood has been tainted. Some Vampires use this power to guard their own herds against poachers or to ward specific vessels against indiscriminate feeding. When you taint the blood of a mortal, roll with Blood. On a 10+, choose 3. On a 7-9, choose 2.
- Any Vampire that partakes of this mortal is unable to heal this session.
- Drinking the blood causes the Vampire excruciating pain (2 harm ap).
- There are no marks giving away suspicion.
- The mortal that has been tainted survives the process no longer than a few days.
- If another Vampire drinks from the poisoned mortal, reduce your Night Circle Status by 1.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Silence of Death: You use the darkness to create a zone of silence around you, or silence certain sounds only. Roll with Mind. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 1 and there is the possibility of complications (the length they are silenced, whether others are silenced as well, whether they are completely silenced, etc.).
- Spend 1 hold to silence everything within Hand range, including yourself. You may then spend 2 hold to increase this range by 1 step (Close, then Close/Far).
- Spend hold on a 1 for 1 basis to silence a single target, including yourself.
 
 
CELERITY
 
Not all Vampires are slow, meticulous creatures. When needed, some Vampires can move fast -- really fast.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Like Lightning: When you need to travel a long distance in an impossibly short amount of time or complete a task in record time, Let It Out but roll with Blood. On a hit, you are winded and take -1 ongoing until rest, or mark a Scar (Drained: -1 Blood) to push through.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Rapid Response: You use Celerity to move with amazing swiftness in times of stress. Your quick reflexes have been elevated to the truly unbelievable. You can reflexively take out or put away an item, pick up an unattended object, or manipulate an item in your possession. When you Turn to Violence on any hit, take an additional option. When you roll to Escape, roll 3 dice and take the best 2 results instead of rolling 2d6.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Faster Than Thought: (requires Rapid Response or Like Lightning) The Embrace gifted you with startling speed and reflexes. You can literally move so fast that you are almost impossible to see or affect as long as you do not slow down or stop. You can run across short gaps, up steep inclines, and even over low walls without pause, though there are dangers associated with moving at such heightened velocity. In addition, you gain +1 Mind (max +3). If you exceed +3 Mind to +4 Mind, erase your Mortalis Circle Status to 0 permanently.
 
 
DEMENTATION
 
The special legacy of the Malkavian Clan, Dementation allows the Vampire to channel madness, focus it, and pour it into the minds of those around them. The practitioner of Dementation need not actually be mad themselves, at least initially, although madness seems to grant certain insight into the key tenets of this Discipline.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Wavering Reality: You have a unique perception of reality. When you Figure Someone Out, replace the existing questions with four of your own, but it is up to the MC to choose which two are answered.
 
[DISCIPLINE] The Haunting: The Vampire manipulates the sensory centers of their victim's brain, flooding the victim's senses with visions, sounds, scents, or feelings that are not really there. Take +1 if you are able to touch the subject. Roll with Mind. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, mark 1 Corruption. On a miss, you are enraptured with the formation and world of your own prank or nightmare; the MC makes a hard move.
- A dreadful, reality-altering nightmare or delusion is wormed into the subject's mind; they are instantly stunned. When stunned, they are unable to take any physical action for a short time. If they do take a physical action, the MC can make a hard move.
- The victim experiences a primal sense of fear as if the very shadows are closing in to suffocate them, and Escapes from the cause of their fears in haste (player dictates what their fear is).
- The victim is unable to face their fear and takes stun-harm; doing anything other than staring is taking a chance.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Eyes of Chaos: This peculiar power allows the Vampire to take advantage of the fleeting clarity hidden in insanity. At the start of every session, you recall a dream carrying encoded information about the night. When you do, roll with Mind. On a hit, the MC gives you a clear impression of what your future has in store based on the dream you describe. On a 10+, you can also ask one question and have the MC answer it. On a 7-9, you get a clear impression only. On a miss, you get only a vague idea of what your dream was about.
 
 
DOMINATE
 
This Discipline involves influencing the very thoughts and actions of others through the Vampire's force of will. Commands must be issued verbally; direct mind-to-mind contact is the purview of Auspex.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Mesmerize: By using your voice to subtly influence a subject, you can verbally implant a hypnotic suggestion in their subconscious mind. Roll with Mind. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 1. On a miss, if the target is in your Circle, they gain 2 hold against you, usable to create Debts or trigger a Circle Turn move.
Spend 1 hold to make the target take one of these actions:
- Speak a few words of your choice.
- Give you something they hold.
- Make a concerted attack on a target of your choice.
- Truthfully answer one simple question.
If you run out of hold the Discipline ends. If the target takes damage you lose 1 hold. While the Discipline is ongoing, to take any other action you must Keep Your Cool.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Ripples of the Heart: This power allows a Vampire to leave a particular emotion, thought, or image within the bloodstream of any mortal from whom they feed. Any other Vampire who subsequently drinks from that mortal experiences those same thoughts as if they were their own. This is an additional option added to Eternal Hunger. When you roll Eternal Hunger, you may also trigger this effect.
 
[DISCIPLINE] The Forgetful Mind: After capturing the subject's gaze, the Vampire delves into the subject's memories, stealing or re-creating them at their whim. Roll with Mind. On a 10+, choose 3 of the following. On a 7-9, choose 2. On a miss, choose 1 but the MC may add complications.
- Alter or remove an entire scene from the subject's memory.
- Instead of lasting 1 day, the memory alteration is permanent.
- The person involved is unaware of your action.
 
 
FORTITUDE
 
All Vampires possess a preternatural constitution that makes most normal damage inconsequential.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Thick Skin: When you activate Fortitude, roll with Spirit. On a 10+, choose both. On a 7-9, choose 1 and mark corruption. On a miss, mark the Scar (Cracked: -1 Spirit) from overexertion.
- Take +1 armor.
- Once in this scene you can reduce harm that you take by one.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Demon Hide: (requires Thick Skin) Once per scene, instead of taking harm from being attacked, you can mark corruption equal to the amount of harm you wish to negate.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Resilience: Your body mends any injury given time. Healing from non-magical sources such as feeding occurs at double the speed of others.
 
 
OBFUSCATE
 
This uncanny power enables Vampires to conceal themselves from others' sight. By simply wishing to remain unseen, a Vampire can disappear, even if they stand in full view of a crowd.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Cloak the Shadows: The Vampire must rely on nearby shadows and cover to assist in hiding their presence. When you step into an out-of-the-way, shadowed place and ease yourself from normal sight, roll with Blood. On a 10+, you become invisible and no one can see you. You can move, but using any action that interacts with another cancels this effect. On a 7-9, choose 1:
- Movement gives you away.
- You are only hidden when in shadows.
- Your own view is heavily clouded.
[ADVANCED] Using Cloak the Shadows, you can extend your coverage for up to 10 people in close range.
If you have Status 3+ in the Night Circle, supernatural beings attuned to the Night Circle (Vampires, Werewolves, Mages) may still sense your unnatural absence even if they cannot see you.
 
[DISCIPLINE] A Thousand Faces: You can make the people around you think you are someone else. Roll with Heart. On a 10+, choose 1. On a 7-9, choose 1; if discovered, reduce your Circle Status by 1 with the Circle you deceived.
- You appear as the same person to everyone. They do not necessarily expect to see you or know who you are, but you do not look like yourself.
- You appear as a different person to everyone who views you. They expect to see you and know who you are impersonating.
If you have Status 3+ in the Night Circle, when you use Obfuscate to vanish or disguise yourself, roll with Blood. On a 10+, no one remembers your presence unless they have supernatural resistance. On a 7-9, you disappear but a faint sense of something missing lingers in the Minds of those affected.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Shadow of Apep: (only for Clan Ministry) These ancient monsters can take the form of Set's defeated enemy, Apep. By taking 1 corruption, the Vampire becomes a serpent of fluid, glittering darkness -- not mere shadow, but anti-light. They are immune to physical attacks but also unable to attack others while in this form.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Ignis Fatuus: The Vampire may conjure a minor mirage that confounds one sense. Roll with Heart. On a 10+, the image is believable; choose 2. On a 7-9, you may mark 1 harm or gain a Scar (Fractured: -1 Mind) to stabilize the illusion.
Choose from:
- Enable the mirage to move within your line of sight.
- The mirage confounds an additional sense.
- The mirage lasts for a short time after you stop concentrating.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Horrid Reality: Rather than create simple illusions, the Vampire projects traumatic hallucinations directly into a victim's Mind. Roll with Heart. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- They end up in another dangerous yet very different situation.
- They suffer harm on the way out of the hallucination.
- You are able to affect a very important memory of their recent history.
- They leave something important behind as they are dazed from the experience.
- You learn how they break under pressure; take a Debt against them.
 
 
OBLIVION
 
Oblivion is a manifestation of the endless void -- a primal darkness that hungers for decay, destruction, and the unraveling of all creation. Wielders of this abyssal power command shadows that twist with malevolent intent and commune with the restless dead, all while risking their souls to the corrupting whispers of the void.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Shroudsight: You pierce the Shroud -- the barrier separating the living from the Underworld -- and perceive its hidden truths. Roll with Mind. On a 10+, ask 2 questions from the Study a Place of Power list or Figure Someone Out (applying to Spectres or the Underworld). You also gain insight into any dangers lurking beyond the Shroud. On a 7-9, ask 1 question but your presence disturbs the dead; choose: mark corruption or owe a Debt to a spirit who notices you. On a miss, a malevolent spirit notices you and may pursue you or demand a price. Using Shroudsight on Spectres may trigger Wild Circle conflicts or Debts owed to the dead.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Compel Soul: This power allows a necromancer to call a ghost back from the Underworld and command or bind them. Roll with Mind. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 1 and mark corruption. On a miss, mark a Scar (Fractured: -1 Mind) from spiritual backlash. Spend hold 1 for 1 to:
- Summon a soul that you knew in life, witnessed in death, or possess an item the soul interacted with. The soul will only stay for a short time.
- Compel a soul to perform a task of less than a session's duration.
- Compel a soul to answer 1 question truthfully.
- Bind the soul to a place or object for one session.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Shadow Play: This power grants the Vampire limited control over shadows and other ambient darkness. Though the Vampire cannot truly create darkness, they can overlap and stretch existing shadows, creating patches of gloom. When you shape and control darkness and shadow, roll with Blood. On a 10+, hold 2. On a 7-9, hold 1 and mark corruption. On a miss, mark corruption and a dark power gains 1 Debt on you. Spend hold 1 for 1 to:
- While hidden, take +1 forward to Mislead, Distract, or Trick.
- Being unseen lets you take +1 forward to Escape.
- In stealth, take +1 forward to Turn to Violence.
- Use your creations to strangle and smother an air-breather (1 harm ap close loud).
 
[DISCIPLINE] Black Metamorphosis: The Vampire calls upon their inner darkness, becoming a monstrous hybrid of matter and shadow. Though still humanoid, the Vampire takes on an almost demonic appearance as the darkness within bubbles to the surface. When you take on the form of a shadow demon full of tentacles and inky darkness, roll with Blood. On a 10+, hold 2. On a 7-9, hold 1 and mark corruption. On a miss, mark Corruption and the dark power gains 1 Debt on you. Spend hold 1 for 1:
- Your tentacles are able to conceal others in their darkness.
- Your tentacles are able to blind the light for a short area.
- Your tentacles deal 2 harm (close, loud) to your victim.
- Your tentacles can grasp a target, pinning them in place.
- Take +1 armor as your form fades in and out of darkness.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Tenebrous Form: At this level, the Kindred's mastery of darkness is so extensive that they may physically become it. By taking 1 corruption, the Vampire becomes an inky, amoeboid patch of shadow. Vampires in this form are practically invulnerable and may slither through cracks and crevices. When you assume Tenebrous Form, mark 1 corruption. The form lasts for the scene or until you choose to end it.
 
 
POTENCE
 
Vampires endowed with this Discipline possess preternatural strength. Potence enables Vampires to leap tremendous distances, lift massive weights, and strike opponents with terrifying force.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Rebel's Ferocity: In combat, the power of your combinations is harder to handle. When you Turn to Violence on a 12+, deal a second injury as if from a second attack of the same severity.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Force of Nature: You are capable of tremendous feats of strength. Most Vampires can throw normal-sized objects ridiculously far and lift incredibly heavy objects. With this power, you can throw large and heavy objects at far distances as well.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Predator's Exertion: (requires Rebel's Ferocity) The Embrace gifted you with startling strength. You are so strong that you can direct your incredible power through the ground or floor to affect enemies at range. By striking the ground, you send a shock wave along the surface, increasing melee attacks to far distances. In addition, you gain +1 Blood (max +3). To increase Blood above +3, erase your Wild Circle Status permanently.
 
 
PRESENCE
 
Vampires who develop Presence can inspire zealous fervor, devoted passion, or unspeakable terror in mortals and immortals alike.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Dread Gaze: While all Vampires can frighten others by physically revealing their true vampiric natures, this power focuses those elements to insanely terrifying levels. When you do, roll with Heart. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 1. At your will, no matter the circumstances, you can spend your hold 1 for 1:
- Inflict 1 harm (ap).
- They take -1 right now.
- You take +1 forward to persuade an NPC or Turn to Violence.
If they fulfill your command, that counts for all your remaining hold. On a miss, you inflict 1 harm (ap) upon your subject to no benefit.
 
[DISCIPLINE] The Eyes of the Serpent: This power grants the Vampire the legendary hypnotic gaze of the serpent. The Vampire's eyes become gold with large black irises, and individuals in the character's vicinity find themselves strangely attracted to them and unable to do anything besides gaze into their eyes. Roll with Mind. On a 10+, hold 2. On a 7-9, hold 1. Spend your hold 1 for 1 to make eye contact with an NPC present, who freezes or flinches and cannot take action until you break it off. On a miss, your enemies identify you immediately as their foremost threat.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Muse: (only for Clan Toreador) When you perform your chosen art, any act of expression or culture, or when you put its product before an audience, roll with Heart. On a 10+, spend 3. On a 7-9, spend 1. Spend 1 to name an NPC member of your audience and choose one:
- This person must meet me.
- This person must have my services.
- This person loves me.
- This person must give me a gift.
- This person admires my patron.
On a miss, you gain no benefit but suffer no harm or lost opportunity. You simply perform very well.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Entrancement: When you have time and solitude with someone, they become fixated upon you. Roll with Heart. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 2. They can spend your hold 1 for 1 by:
- Giving you something you want.
- Acting as your eyes and ears.
- Fighting to protect you.
- Doing something you tell them to.
For NPCs, while you have hold over them they cannot act against you. For PCs, any time you like you can spend your hold 1 for 1:
- They Distract themselves with the thought of you and must Keep Their Cool.
- They inspire themselves with the thought of you and take +1 forward right now.
On a miss, they hold 2 over you on the exact same terms.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Majesty: (requires Dread Gaze or Entrancement) Under Majesty's influence, hearts break, power trembles, and the bold shake. The Vampire can augment their supernatural presence to overwhelming effect. When you impose your will on a crowd, roll with Heart. On a 10+, choose 2. On a 7-9, choose 1.
- They do what you want.
- They fight as a gang for you.
- None of them fight you back.
- None of them Escape.
On a miss, the crowd retaliates, triggering a Circle Turn event.
 
 
PROTEAN
 
Vampires who develop this Discipline can grow bestial claws, assume the forms of wolves and bats, transform into mist, and meld into the earth.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Feral Claws: The Vampire's nails transform into long, bestial claws. Treat your claws and fangs as 3 harm (hand natural) that can destroy or at least scratch most non-metal -- and even some metal -- objects.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Cobra Fangs: You gain a venomous bite along with a serpentine form. This power enables a Vampire to gain the deadly bite without the full-body transformation, making it more useful for taking victims by surprise. When you inflict harm, inflict +1 harm.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Shape of the Beast: You can take the form of a bat or wolf. Roll with Blood. On a 10+, you shift fine. On a 7-9, choose 1. On a miss, mark a Scar (Bestial: -1 Heart) or lose 1 Mortalis Circle Status.
- You drew upon the darkness to change. Mark corruption.
- The change was painful. Mark 1 harm (ap).
- Your secret is revealed. Take -1 Rapport against the closest non-Night Circle around.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Mist Form: This truly unsettling power enables the Vampire to turn into mist. Their physical shape disperses into a hazy cloud, still subject entirely to the immortal's will. While you are in Mist Form, you cannot interact with anything and nothing can interact with you. If you are using Mist Form to Escape, treat the 10+ as the advanced 12+.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Mask of Clay: Schooled in the basics of fleshcrafting, you can change your own appearance. When you take another humanoid form, roll with Mind. On a 10+, you have got it just right and can stay that way as long as you desire, provided you have Figured them out as noted below. On a 7-9, choose one:
- Your impersonation does not hold up under scrutiny.
- Assuming the disguise is an agonizing process. Take 1 harm (ap).
- The disguise will not last very long.
Add to the list of options for Figure Someone Out: "You have studied their appearance and mannerisms and can duplicate them."
 
[DISCIPLINE] Horrid Form: (requires Shape of the Beast or Mask of Clay) Vampires use this power to become hideous and deadly monsters. The Vampire's stature increases in height, the skin becomes a sickly greenish-gray or grayish-black chitin, the arms become apelike and ropy with ragged black nails, and the face warps into something out of a nightmare. When you alter your body into that of a monster, roll with Blood. On a 10+, hold 3. On a 7-9, hold 1 (or hold 2 and mark corruption). On a miss, mark a Scar (Grotesque: -1 Heart) from permanent disfigurement. Spend your hold 1 for 1:
- Alter 1 of your appendages into a vile shape or gross form, also altering its size and density.
- One target must Keep Their Cool or flee in haste.
- Your skin has altered, giving you +1 armor.
- You are now stronger; you have +2 harm weapons (hand natural) and knock down objects in your path with ease.
- You are faster, giving you +1 to Escape and allowing you to keep pace with fast-moving methods of transportation.
 
[DISCIPLINE] Blood of Acid: At this level of mastery, the Vampire has converted their blood to a viscous acid. Any blood they consume likewise becomes acid, corrosive enough to burn human and vampiric flesh as well as materials like wood. Any drop of this blood can melt many items in your path and damages opponents (1 harm ap).
 
[DISCIPLINE] Breath of the Dragon: The Vampire becomes like one of the terrible draculs of the Old World, able to exhale a deadly gout of flame. This flame does not hurt the Vampire themselves, though they may become trapped in flames if it engulfs flammable objects. You can wield this fire as a weapon (2 harm close area fire). Each time you do, mark corruption.
