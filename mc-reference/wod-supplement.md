# City of Shadow — World of Darkness Supplement v2

**Source:** WoD Supplement v2 (custom supplement for this campaign)  
**Maintained:** `mc-reference/wod-supplement.md`

This supplement overlays World of Darkness lore on Urban Shadows 2e. It adds the Instinct Die, Extreme Failures by playbook, altered moves, and eight WoD-specific extensions.

> **Core rules** are in `mc-reference/rules-reference.md`. This file covers only what the supplement adds or replaces.

---

## 1. The Instinct Die — Always Active

When any move is rolled, one of the two d6 is the **Instinct Die.** Use a different color die to track it.

**The Instinct Die result is included in the 2d6 total.** It is one of the two regular dice, tracked by color — not a separate roll.

### Extreme Failure Trigger

When the **total roll is 6 or lower** AND **the Instinct Die shows a 1**: trigger an **Extreme Failure.**

- The MC chooses which Extreme Failure fits — or the player may suggest one.
- It should shift the narrative significantly — not just add harm. Change the situation.
- See Section 2 for the full list by playbook.

- Loose Lips — reveal crucial information to the wrong person

- Twitchy Trigger Finger — attack before confirming target; hit unintended victim
- Exhausted — push too hard; −1 ongoing until rest

**The Veteran**
- Old Wounds Resurface — past trauma distracts; enemies gain advantage

## 3. Altered Moves

These moves **replace** their US2e versions entirely for the relevant playbooks.

### Channeling (replaces Wizard's Let It Out)

When you call upon magical power, roll with **Spirit.**

- **10+:** Hold 3 Spell-hold and choose 1 cost.
- **7–9:** Hold 2 Spell-hold and choose 1 cost.
- **Miss:** Gain 1 Spell-hold; MC chooses two costs; power draws immediate Circle attention.

**Costs (choose 1):** −1 ongoing to Circle moves until rest | unexpected dangerous side effects (MC decides) | suffer 1-harm (ap) from backlash | mark corruption

*Spell-hold is spent to activate specific magical effects from the Mage: The Awakening extension.*

---

### Manifestation Transformation (replaces Spectre's manifest)

When incorporeal and wishing to manifest, roll with **Spirit.**

- **10+:** Take any of your Shade's Manifestation Forms.
- **7–9:** Take your Shade's First or Second Form.
- **Miss:** Too weak — First Form only. Can spend 2-harm to bump up Form by 1 level.

---

### Daystar Burns! (replaces Vamp's blood magic corruption move)

When stepping into sunlight: mark corruption and take 1-harm (ap). If exposed beyond a few moments, Let It Out with Blood to push through the agony. On a miss, mark a Scar (Shattered: −1 Blood).

---

### Web (replaces Vamp's original Web)

When someone outside your Coterie (Vampire group) seeks help, advice, or protection, they are added to your shared Web. All Coterie members gain advantage when leveraging Debts against those in the Web. This shared Web is only active while you remain unified as the Night Circle.

---

### Blink (replaces Spectre's nightmare)

Mark trauma to instantly teleport to any place you have been before, riding the dark winds of the dead. If you bring someone with you, mark an additional trauma.

## 4. WoD Extension Chapters

The supplement adds WoD-specific rules for eight game lines, each extending its corresponding US2e playbook.

| Chapter | Game Line | Base Playbook | Key Mechanics |
|---------|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| CH3 | Changeling: The Lost | The Fae | Kiths; Dual Kith; Multi-Kith; Triumphal Fate |
| CH4 | Demon: The Descent | The Tainted | Demonic form; Going Loud; Self-Destruct |
| CH5 | Hunter: The Vigil | The Hunter | Predator's Kin; Tactical Advantage; Compacts; Gospels |
| CH6 | Mage: The Awakening | The Wizard | Expanded Channeling; Geomancer; Shadow Name; Manifold Presence |
| CH7 | Orpheus | The Spectre | Blink; Contaminate; Wail; Manifestation Forms |
| CH8 | Slasher | Any Mortalis | Ripper, Brute, or Genius paths; unique advancement |
| CH9 | Vampire: The Masquerade | The Vamp | Clan disciplines; Blood Bond; Embrace; Diablerie |
| CH10 | Werewolf: The Forsaken | The Wolf | Packs; Spirit Walk; Predator's Kin; Auspice/Tribe |

### Changeling: The Lost (The Fae)
Players choose a Kith, which defines their supernatural nature and grants specific moves. Dual Kith and Multi-Kith moves allow expansion. Triumphal Fate is a unique advancement path. Key moves include Skitterskulk, Cuckoo's Ruse, Frozen Moment, Slipknot Dreams.

### Demon: The Descent (The Tainted)
Adds demonic form mechanics. Going Loud reveals your demon nature for dramatic effect. Self-Destruct is an extreme last resort. Key moves include Open and Shut Case, Play On Words, Visions of Heaven and Hell, Identity Theft, Trojan Horse.

### Hunter: The Vigil (The Hunter)
Adds Compacts and Conspiracies. Gospel moves (Agares, Amon, Beleth) represent ancient hunting orders. Key moves include Predator's Kin, Tactical Advantage, Blessed Item, Disbelief, Blood of the Cobra, Flashing a Badge, Inquisition.

### Mage: The Awakening (The Wizard)
Significantly expands Channeling with Spell-hold spending. Awakening Paths (Acanthus, Mastigos, Moros, Obrimos, Thyrsus) shape magical style. Key moves include Geomancer, Shadow Name, Banish Spirit, Manifold Presence, Shared Fate, Channel Paradox, Fortune's Favor.

### Orpheus (The Spectre)
Full rework of Spectre's supernatural mechanics. Shade type defines Manifestation Forms (Banshee, Haunter, Poltergeist, etc.). Key moves include Contaminate, Wail, Inhabit, Sandman, Skinrider, Doppelganger. The Blink altered move replaces nightmare.

### Slasher (Any Mortalis)
Add-on extension for The Aware, The Hunter, The Sworn, or The Veteran. Three paths: Ripper (pure violence), Brute (overwhelming force), Genius (traps and cunning). Has its own advancement chart. Key moves include Strength from the Tales, Unconquerable Challenge, Compelling Madness, Trap Master.

### Vampire: The Masquerade (The Vamp)
Adds Clan system (Lasombra, Nosferatu, Gangrel, Toreador, Tremere, Salubri, etc.). Each Clan has unique Disciplines. Key mechanics include Blood Bond, Embrace, Diablerie, Coterie/Web system, Tenebrous Form, Eternal Hunger, Dominate, Aura Perception, Feral Whispers. Daystar Burns! replaces blood magic corruption move.

### Werewolf: The Forsaken (The Wolf)
Adds Pack mechanics for cooperative play. Auspice (moon phase at first change) and Tribe shape character role. Key moves include Spirit Walk, Predator's Kin, Eternal Hunter, Leech Rage, Luna's Dictum, Voice of Command, Shadow Step, Rage Armor, Battle Mind, Fog of War.

## 6. XP System (WoD Supplement Version)

*This campaign uses the WoD Supplement XP system, not standard US2e.*

Mark Experience when you:
- Use any move requiring an action (basic, Circle, Debt, archetype, add-on, or corruption move)
- Mark a Debt with each PC
- Leverage influence and engage with each Circle type

**Exceptions:**
- Wizard Channeling and Fae magic moves: mark XP for each unique spell or faerie power used
- WoD Extension Traits: mark XP when demonstrated consistently in play

A move can only grant XP once per advancement cycle.
